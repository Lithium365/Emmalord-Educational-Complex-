Welcome to the Student Representative Council Page

The Student Representative Council (SRC) is the voice of the student body, a dynamic group of elected students dedicated to making a positive impact on the school community. Our primary goal is to represent the diverse needs and concerns of students and work collaboratively to enhance the overall school experience.

Our Mission

Our mission is rooted in fostering a sense of unity and inclusivity among students. We aim to create a platform that encourages open communication, active engagement, and the implementation of initiatives that directly benefit our fellow students. Through various projects and activities, we strive to contribute to the vibrant and thriving school environment that defines our institution.

What We Do

The SRC engages in a wide range of activities, including organizing events, advocating for student concerns, and collaborating with school administration to address important issues. From planning exciting school festivals to spearheading community service projects, we are committed to creating a positive and impactful school experience for every student.
